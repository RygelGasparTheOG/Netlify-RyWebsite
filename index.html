About Me

My name is Zyan Rygel Gaspar, I write stuff such as short novels in my free time. I am a passionate storyteller who believes in the power of words to transport readers to new worlds. My journey as a writer began when I was in Grade 7, I was writing short stories that I lost due to accidentally throwing my previous works in the trash, since then I've been writing short novels in my free time. I've been crafting tales that explore the depths of human emotion, adventure, and the extraordinary within the ordinary.

When I'm not writing, you can find me playing the piano. I draw inspiration from Charlotte Bronte, Frances Hodson Burnett, and Mark Twain's works and believe that every person has a story worth telling.

My writing style focuses on dialogue heavy narratives. I'm particularly drawn to themes of romance and love creating stories that make readers think long after they've turned the final page.
